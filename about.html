About WeatherApp

WeatherApp is designed to provide real-time weather information for any city across the globe. Whether you're planning your day or just curious about the weather, our app gives you instant access to accurate data about temperature, humidity, and more.

Key Features:

  • Get weather data by entering any city name.
  • View real-time temperature, humidity, and weather conditions.
  • Simple and easy-to-use interface.
  • Access global weather data powered by OpenWeather API.

WeatherApp fetches the latest weather data from the OpenWeather API, ensuring that you always have up-to-date information at your fingertips. Start by entering your city name, and we'll take care of the rest!
